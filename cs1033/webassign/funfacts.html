Fun Facts

Here are 3 birds from Ontario that I find interesting/beautiful/fascinating.

Hummingbirds

  1. Hummingbirds are the only birds that can fly backwards.
  2. They have the highest metabolism of any bird, with their heart beating up to 1,260 times per minute.
  3. Some hummingbirds can flap their wings up to 80 times per second.

Emperor Penguins

  1. Penguins are flightless birds that are perfectly adapted to life in the water.
  2. Emperor penguins can dive deeper than any other bird, reaching depths of up to 1,800 feet.
  3. Penguins form strong bonds with their mates, and will often mate for life.

Bald Eagle

  1. Bald eagles are a symbol of the United States, and are found only in North America.
  2. They have a wingspan of up to 7 feet, making them one of the largest birds of prey in the world.
  3. Bald eagles are skilled hunters, and can dive at speeds of up to 100 miles per hour when catching prey.
